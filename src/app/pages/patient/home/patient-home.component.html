How to Book an Appointment

{{ step.title }}

Step 1: Choose a Doctor

Browse our list of qualified healthcare providers and select one who meets your needs.

You can filter doctors by specialty, availability, or location to find the perfect match for your healthcare needs.

Step 2: Fill Up the Appointment Form

Provide your personal information and details about your health concerns.

The information you provide helps your doctor prepare for your appointment and ensures you receive the best care possible.

Step 3: Scan GCash Reference Number

Use your GCash app to scan the QR code and make your consultation fee payment.

Our secure payment system ensures your transaction is safe and immediately processed.

Step 4: Upload Payment Receipt

Take a screenshot or photo of your payment confirmation and upload it to verify your payment.

This confirmation helps us validate your payment quickly and proceed with your appointment booking.

Step 5: Wait for Confirmation

Once your booking is processed, you'll receive a confirmation notification.

You'll receive an email and SMS with your appointment details, including date, time, and doctor information.
